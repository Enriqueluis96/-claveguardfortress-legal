ClaveGuard Fortress

Local cold vault for critical secrets, recovery codes, private documents, and continuity planning.

ClaveGuard Fortress is designed for people who want selected high-value secrets to remain local: no account, no cloud backend, no telemetry, and no Android Internet permission.

No accountThere is no login service or provider-side vault account.
No cloud backendVault data stays on the device unless the user manually exports an encrypted backup.
No telemetryThe current release does not include analytics, telemetry, ads, or crash reporting SDKs.
Encrypted local vaultSensitive vault content and document attachments are encrypted locally before persistence.

Important limitations

ClaveGuard Fortress does not provide remote password reset, provider-side recovery, or absolute security guarantees. Device compromise, malware, root or jailbreak, lost recovery material, and lost backups remain real risks.
